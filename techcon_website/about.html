Our History

TechCon began in 2011 as a small gathering of local developers in a co-working space in Austin, Texas. Founded by a group of passionate engineers and educators, the goal was simple: to create a space where people could share real-world experiences about emerging technology.The very first TechCon featured just six speakers, one whiteboard, and a crowd of 75 curious minds. Topics ranged from mobile app development to early experiments with AI, long before it became mainstream.

Over the years, TechCon has grown exponentially. By 2015, it had expanded to include over 50 speakers and attracted more than 1,500 attendees from around the world. The event moved to larger venues, introduced workshops, and began live-streaming sessions to reach a global audience.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower innovators, creators, and learners by building a global platform where technology meets possibility.We strive to spark meaningful conversations, inspire groundbreaking ideas, and foster a diverse and inclusive tech community.Through immersive experiences, hands-on learning, and open collaboration, we aim to shape a future where technology enhances lives and solves real-world challenges.
